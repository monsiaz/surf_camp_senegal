Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 01, 2025

NGOR SURF CAMP (hereinafter referred to as "we", "our" or "the Company") values the privacy of its visitors and customers. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store and protect your personal information when you visit our website https://www.surfcampsenegal.com/.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal data:

  • Identification information: name, email address, phone number, postal address.
  • Booking and payment information: details required to process reservations and payments.
  • Technical data: IP address, device type, browser, browsing behaviour, pages visited.
  • Voluntary information you provide through contact forms, newsletters, reviews, etc.

2. How We Collect Your Information

We collect information when:

  • You make a booking or contact us through our forms.
  • You subscribe to our newsletter.
  • You complete a payment through our secure payment providers.
  • Cookies and analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) track your navigation on our website.

3. Purpose of Data Collection

Your personal information is collected and used for:

  • Processing reservations and providing our services.
  • Communicating important information regarding your stay.
  • Customer support and service follow-up.
  • Sending promotional offers and newsletters (if you have consented).
  • Compliance with applicable legal and regulatory obligations.

4. Data Storage, Sharing and Security

Your personal data is securely stored on our technical providers' servers. We only share your information with trusted third-party service providers (e.g., payment processors, hosting providers, email services) as required to deliver our services. Data may be disclosed if required by law or to respond to legal requests.

5. Communication with Users

We may contact you by email, phone, SMS, or postal mail to:

  • Confirm and manage your bookings.
  • Provide updates or practical information regarding your stay.
  • Send marketing and promotional content (if you have agreed).

6. Cookies and Tracking Tools

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies in order to:

  • Improve site performance and navigation.
  • Analyse traffic and visitor behaviour.
  • Personalise content and offers.

You may configure your browser to refuse cookies or to alert you when cookies are being used.

7. Withdrawing Consent

You may at any time:

  • Request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data.
  • Withdraw your consent for marketing communications.
