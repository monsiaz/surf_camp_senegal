At Ngor Surfcamp Teranga, we mix world-class waves with laid-back island vibes and expert surf coaching that actually moves the needle.

Whether you are chasing your first green wave or refining your turns, we guide your progression at your rhythm in the heart of West Africa’s surf culture.

At Ngor Surfcamp, your surf journey goes beyond the waves. With expert coaching and a relaxed island lifestyle, we help you find your best surf and your flow.

Surf better and feel connected: two sessions a day, morning and afternoon, taken to the best spot according to forecast and swell. Our surf guide Abu, rated the best surf guide in Dakar, knows exactly how to place you on the wave. Fast progression guaranteed.

From sunrise surf to sunset chill, you will grow in and out of the water, surrounded by people who share the same passion.

Your level at Ngor

Your level at Ngor

Beginner

Never surfed, or just tried once or twice. You will start on a foam board on Ngor Left, a forgiving wave perfect for learning. By the end of your stay, you will be standing and riding.

Intermediate

You can consistently pop up and ride down the line. Ready to work on turns, reading waves, and timing your take-off. Ngor Right on smaller days will become your playground.

Advanced

You surf turns and are pushing your performance. Ngor Right at full size is your wave: powerful, fast and demanding. Video analysis will fast-track your specific weaknesses.
