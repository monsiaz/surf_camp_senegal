Ngor Island

Two sides, two moods

Ngor Island is one of the most special places to stay in Dakar for surfers, travelers and anyone looking for a laid-back Atlantic escape. Just a short boat ride from the mainland, this small island blends beautiful ocean views, easy beach access and a calm local atmosphere that makes every stay feel unique.

Legendary Surf: Ngor Right

One of West Africa's most famous waves, made legendary by The Endless Summer (1964). Consistent, powerful point break for intermediate to advanced surfers.

Street art, colorful houses, art galleries and cozy cafes. Ngor has a unique soul far from mass tourism.
